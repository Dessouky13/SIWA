## TOUR PROGRAMS (6) — real itineraries

### P1 — Siwa, 1 Night / 2 Days ("Discover the Magic of Siwa")
Transfer from Cairo in the evening — by private car OR coach (client's choice).
**Day 1 — Discovering Siwa's magic:** Arrival at Siwa Oasis; hotel check-in; rest after the journey. Visit the Temple of the Oracle of Amun (where Alexander the Great once sought answers amid the desert's silence) — from above, a sweeping panorama of the oasis, shimmering lakes, palm and olive groves to the horizon. Visit Cleopatra's Spring (natural water among the palms). 4x4 safari into the heart of the desert: Siwa nature reserve; photo stops; dunes and sandboarding; Bedouin tea with locals; sunset in the desert; Bedouin dinner under the open sky; stargazing. Return to hotel.
**Day 2 — Between salt and history:** Salt lakes (float effortlessly in the turquoise, mineral-rich water). Natural hot springs. Deep-relaxation session inside the salt cave. Siwan Museum (local life, houses, customs, dress). Shali Fortress — the old mud-brick town, walking the ancient alleys. Sunset on one of Siwa's beautiful islands. Return to Cairo in the evening.

### P2 — Siwa, 2 Nights / 3 Days
Transfer from Cairo morning or evening (car/coach). Itinerary adjustable to the client.
**Day 1:** Arrival, hotel check-in, rest. Tours: Temple of Amun; Cleopatra's Spring; a beautiful Siwa island; sunset amid stunning nature. Candlelit dinner at a restaurant. Return to hotel.
**Day 2:** Salt lakes; natural hot springs; salt-cave relaxation session. 4x4 safari into the desert: special Siwan lunch cooked in the heart of the desert; Siwa nature reserve; dunes, gravel fields and mountains; photo stops; Bedouin tea with locals; sandboarding; desert sunset; Bedouin dinner under the open sky; stargazing. Return to hotel.
**Day 3:** Breakfast on one of Siwa's beautiful islands. Visit Shali Fortress and wander the old alleys. Departure at 10:30 pm back to Cairo.

### P3 — Siwa, 4 Days / 3 Nights ("To Live the Legend, Not Just Visit It")
**Day 1 — Crossing into the land of secrets & the first sunset:** Arrival & relaxation at the hotel, breathing the pure oasis air of olive and palm. Temple of the Oracle of Amun (in the footsteps of Alexander the Great) + full panorama of Siwa as a green sea fighting the sand. Cleopatra's Spring — the ancient royal legend, warm natural water among fruiting palms. Sunset sail to one of Siwa's enchanting islands; sit in silence as the sun sinks into the lake. Warm Siwan dinner in the calm oasis air. Return to hotel.
**Day 2 — The Great Safari, where stories are born in the heart of the sand:** Traditional breakfast amid pristine nature on a Siwa island. 10:30 am — 4x4 vehicles set off into the Great Sand Sea (a full day). Real thrill crossing the golden, wind-sculpted dunes. A very special Bedouin "special" lunch cooked on charcoal in the authentic way. Sandboarding on the soft sand. The majestic desert sunset as the sands turn deep gold. As night falls, gather around the campfire (raqya) for a lavish Bedouin dinner, followed by Siwan mint tea under a perfectly clear, star-studded sky. Return to hotel.
**Day 3 — White healing, the scent of history, and luminous Shali:** Salt lakes — float effortlessly thanks to the high salinity; a physical experience that releases every negative energy. Hot springs + deep relaxation inside the salt cave under soft candlelight. Mountain of the Dead — Pharaonic and Greek tombs carved into the mountain. Shali Fortress and the story of "kershef" (salt-saturated mud brick); watch the sunset from atop the fortress. Free time in the old market for dates, olives and Siwan handicrafts. Finale: an exceptional candlelit dinner on the Shali rooftop. Return to hotel.
**Day 4 — Farewell, returning with a new heart:** Breakfast at the hotel; one last look at the oasis. Check-out and departure, carrying a Siwan calm that stays with you for years.

### P4 — Siwa, 5 Nights / 6 Days ("Nature, Relaxation & Meditation Retreat")
Designed for lovers of nature, quiet and simple living — salt lakes, natural springs, open desert and authentic Siwan culture.
**Day 1 — Arrival & relaxation:** Arrival and hotel check-in; rest. Visit one of the natural hot springs; warm water. Time to relax and meditate in the calm oasis. Healthy dinner. Overnight.
**Day 2 — Salt lakes & Shali Fortress:** Traditional Siwan breakfast on a beautiful natural island. Salt lakes (natural floating). A hot spring for relaxation. Shali Fortress, the old town; walk the historic alleys, learn the oasis's history and culture. Sunset from atop the fortress. A special dinner amid heritage ambience and candlelight. Return to hotel.
**Day 3 — Great Sand Sea safari:** Full-day desert safari. Explore the golden dunes and enchanting landscapes. Meditation and quiet in the desert nature. Healthy, special Siwan lunch. Sunset in the heart of the desert. Bedouin dinner under the open sky. Stargazing and clear night skies. Return to hotel.
**Day 4 — Relaxation & nature day:** Salt lakes. A natural-oil care session. Hot springs to relax and renew. Return to hotel and rest. A calm boat trip to one of Siwa's natural islands. Sunset amid stunning nature. Return to hotel.
**Day 5 — Cleopatra's Spring & Temple of Amun:** Cleopatra's Spring and its natural surroundings. Salt-cave relaxation session. Free time. Temple of the Oracle of Amun. A natural mountain for a panoramic view over the oasis. Sunset. Dinner. Return to hotel.
**Day 6 — Departure:** Breakfast. Hotel check-out. Return to Cairo with unforgettable memories.

### P5 — Bahariya Oasis & White Desert, 1 Night / 2 Days
**Day 1:** 07:00 depart Cairo. 11:00 arrive Bahariya Oasis. Lunch. Start the 4x4 desert safari: Black Desert; Bedouin Village; Crystal Mountain; Agabat Valley; Mushroom Rock; Old White Desert; New White Desert and its amazing natural rock formations. Sunset view in the White Desert. Arrive at the camping site. Traditional Bedouin dinner (grilled chicken); Bedouin tea; campfire gathering; stargazing. Overnight in a tent (one tent for every two people).
**Day 2:** Sunrise in the desert. Breakfast at the camp. Morning exploration of more rock formations and unique landscapes in the White Desert. Return to Bahariya Oasis. 10:00 depart Bahariya towards Cairo.

### P6 — White Desert, 3 Days / 2 Nights
**Day 1:** Departure to the desert. Black Desert; Cold Sulfur Spring; Bedouin Village; Crystal Mountain. Return to Bahariya Oasis. Dinner. Overnight at the hotel in Bahariya Oasis.
**Day 2:** Full-day desert safari: El Agabat area; sandboarding; Moon Cave; Valley of the Palm; Valley Camps; New White Desert; Old White Desert. Bedouin dinner. Overnight camping in the New White Desert; enjoy the stars and the peaceful desert atmosphere.
**Day 3:** Sunrise in the White Desert. Breakfast. Explore more of the White Desert formations. Return to Bahariya Oasis. Departure.
**Included:** 4x4 Jeep vehicle; breakfast; lunch; dinner; mineral water; fruits; hot & soft drinks; camping equipment (tent, mattresses, blankets & sleeping bags); English-speaking guide; one night hotel accommodation in Bahariya Oasis; one night camping in the White Desert; all meals during the tour.
